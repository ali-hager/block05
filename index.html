+ San Diego Zoo Animals 101

Our Animals

Greetings from the San Diego Zoo team! As a dedicated member of our zoo family, we invite you to delve into the captivating world of our incredible animal residents. From the towering grace of giraffes to the regal charm of lions, and the unique allure of American alligators, Chinese alligators, and spectacled caimans, your role is vital in ensuring our guests get to know and appreciate these fascinating creatures. Join us in sharing their stories and vibrant personalities as we continue to make the San Diego Zoo a place of wonder and education for all our visitors.

Monkeys
  1. Cookie
    2. - A capuchin Monkey recognized her charming face adorned with tufts of hair resembling a hood, small and agile with a prehensile tail
  2. Earl
    3. - A Howler Monkey distinguished by his prehensile tail and robust body, known for his large throat pouch that amplifies his distinctive and resonant howling calls
  3. Banana Pudding
    4. - A Mandrill, vibrant facial colors, elongated snout, and distinctive ridged nose, the largest and most colorful monkey, creating a visually striking appearance in their native rainforest habitats

  2. Aspen
    3. - Petite and critically endangered, Apsen's tapered snout and subtle yellow stripes portray a delicate charm, reflecting her rare and vulnerable status in the wetlands
  3. Mika
    4. - Mika stands out with her broad snout and the distinctive "spectacles" around her eyes, giving her a charismatic and slightly mischievous appearance in the riverside habitat
